## Section 2 · Our Story

**Section Title:** Born in Sfax. Built for Tunisia.

Sécurité Services Plus was founded in Sfax in 2001 by **Karim BEN SALEM** — a decision rooted not just in business ambition, but in a deep family commitment to industrial safety.

The company was built on a foundation shaped by **Mr. Mohamed BEN SALEM**, Karim's father and a distinguished expert in Health, Safety and Environment (HSE) — particularly in fire-fighting within the Oil & Gas sector. His decades of field expertise and technical knowledge became the cornerstone of SS PLUS's technical culture: rigorous, experienced, and uncompromising on standards.

From its early days focused on fire-fighting equipment supply, SS PLUS grew steadily into a comprehensive industrial safety partner. In 2010, the company achieved **ISO 9001 certification** — a milestone that formalized the quality management systems already embedded in its operations. By 2024, SS PLUS took its most significant technological leap with the launch of **WAVE PLUS**, a proprietary digital platform that brings full real-time management to every aspect of the SS PLUS service relationship.

Today, SS PLUS serves a diverse portfolio of B2B clients across Tunisia — from multinational Oil & Gas operators to Tunisian food manufacturers, logistics companies, and public institutions — all united by the trust they place in one partner for their safety needs.
