## Service 01 · Inspection & Maintenance

**Section Title:** Keep Your Safety Systems Performing — Always.

Fire-fighting equipment that fails in a moment of emergency is not a safety system. It is a liability. The cornerstone of the SS PLUS service offer is a rigorous, scheduled inspection and maintenance program that ensures every extinguisher, hose, and fire-fighting system under our care is always ready, always compliant.

### What's Included

**Regular Inspection**  
Periodic compliance checks of all fire extinguishers, fire hoses, and fire-fighting systems — verifying pressure, charge levels, pin integrity, labels, and positioning. Inspection reports issued and archived for regulatory traceability.

**Maintenance & Recharging**  
Full servicing of all extinguisher types — recharging dry powder, CO₂, foam, and water mist units. Valve replacement, O-ring inspection, cylinder integrity testing, and component renewal.

**Fire-Fighting System Maintenance**  
Scheduled maintenance of fixed suppression systems, sprinkler networks, hydrant infrastructure, fire cabinets, and detection systems — ensuring full operational readiness and conformity with Tunisian and international standards.

**Emergency Interventions**  
Rapid response maintenance for urgent equipment failures, damaged systems, or post-incident restoration. Our emergency line is available 24/7 for existing clients.

**Conformity Documentation**  
All maintenance activities are documented with official inspection records, renewal certificates, and compliance reports — ready for regulatory audits, insurance reviews, or internal HSE reporting.

## Service 02 · Safety Consulting

**Section Title:** Expert Guidance. Tailored to Your Reality.

No two industrial sites carry the same risk profile. SS PLUS provides expert HSE consulting services designed to identify the specific hazards in your environment, assess your current safety posture, and build a concrete, actionable protection plan.

### What's Included

**Site Safety Audits**  
Comprehensive on-site evaluation of your facility — hazard identification, existing equipment assessment, gap analysis against Tunisian regulations and international standards, and a written audit report with prioritized recommendations.

**Fire Risk Assessment**  
Systematic fire hazard mapping of your premises — identifying ignition sources, fuel types, exposure areas, and weak points in your existing fire protection coverage. Actionable remediation plan included.

**Safety Management Plan Development**  
For companies building or restructuring their safety infrastructure, we develop complete safety management frameworks — covering emergency procedures, equipment specifications, inspection calendars, and staff responsibility assignments.

**Regulatory Compliance Support**  
Guidance on Tunisian safety regulations, APSAD technical standards, and applicable European norms (EN, ISO 7010, etc.) for your sector. We help you understand your obligations and build a credible compliance record.

**Supplier & Equipment Specification Consulting**  
Independent technical advice on fire-fighting equipment specifications, PPE standards, and safety infrastructure choices — helping you make procurement decisions based on certified technical criteria, not sales pressure.

## Service 03 · Training Programs

**Section Title:** Equip Your People. Not Just Your Site.

Equipment protects nothing if the people around it don't know how to use it. SS PLUS delivers practical, scenario-based safety training programs designed for Tunisian industrial environments — translating technical knowledge into real-world response capability.

### What's Included

**Fire Safety Awareness Training**  
Introduction to fire behavior, fire classes, extinguisher types, and the basics of fire prevention — for all staff levels. Available in Arabic and French.

**Practical Fire Extinguisher Use**  
Hands-on training using real extinguishers in controlled scenarios — participants learn to identify the right extinguisher, assess the situation, and execute the PASS technique correctly under simulated pressure.

**First Responder & Emergency Response Drills**  
Site-specific emergency simulation exercises — fire evacuation drills, alarm response, first aid integration, and first responder role assignments. Debriefed with documented improvement points.

**Fire Warden Certification Program**  
A structured program for employees designated as fire wardens — covering warden duties, site-specific emergency procedures, evacuation coordination, communication protocols, and post-incident reporting.

**HSE Awareness Programs**  
Broader workplace safety awareness sessions covering PPE usage, hazard recognition, incident reporting culture, and safe work practices — tailored to your sector and operational context.

**Customized Training Modules**  
For companies with specific regulatory requirements, sector-specific hazards (Oil & Gas, food processing, chemical environments), or multi-site training needs — SS PLUS develops custom programs with defined learning objectives and competency assessments.
