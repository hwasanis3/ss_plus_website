### Category A · Fire Extinguishers

**The foundation of any fire safety system.**

SS PLUS supplies the complete range of certified fire extinguishers — from standard portable units for office and light industrial use, to specialized extinguishers for the most demanding industrial environments. Every unit we supply is sourced from qualified manufacturers, inspected before delivery, and supported by our full maintenance and recharging service.

**Portable Fire Extinguishers**

| Type | Suitable For |
|------|-------------|
| **ABC Dry Powder** | Solid, liquid, and gas fires — the universal industrial choice |
| **CO₂ (Carbon Dioxide)** | Electrical panels, server rooms, sensitive equipment |
| **Foam (AFFF)** | Flammable liquid fires — fuel stores, workshops |
| **Water Mist** | Class A fires — offices, public buildings |
| **Class D (Metal Powder)** | Metal fires — magnesium, titanium, aluminum machining environments |
| **Class K (Wet Chemical)** | Kitchen and catering fires — cooking oils and fats |
| **Clean Agent (HFC/Novec)** | Archives, data centers, sensitive equipment rooms |

**Mobile Fire Extinguishers**
- Heavy-duty wheeled units from 25kg to 100kg
- Dry powder, CO₂, and foam configurations
- For industrial yards, warehouses, aircraft hangars, and large open-floor facilities

**Fire Extinguisher Accessories**
- Wall brackets and floor stands
- Safety signage and location markers (EN ISO 7010 compliant)
- Anti-theft locks and security brackets
- Inspection tags and conformity labels

### Category B · Fire-Fighting Systems & Equipment

**Fixed systems for comprehensive site protection.**

Beyond portable extinguishers, SS PLUS designs, supplies, and installs complete fire-fighting infrastructure for industrial facilities — from detection and alarm through to suppression and evacuation.

**Fire Detection & Alarm Systems**
- Smoke detectors, heat detectors, flame detectors
- Manual call points and alarm sounders
- Central control panels and addressable systems

**Fixed Suppression Systems**
- Sprinkler systems (wet pipe, dry pipe, pre-action)
- Gaseous suppression systems (CO₂, FM-200, Novec 1230)
- Foam systems for fuel storage and aircraft facilities
- Water mist systems for high-value asset protection

**Fire Hoses & Accessories**
- Semi-rigid and flat hose reels
- Fire hydrant cabinets and landing valves
- Hose couplings, nozzles, and branch pipes
- Storz and instantaneous coupling types

**Fire Doors & Passive Protection**
- Fire-rated door sets and closers
- Intumescent seals and fire stopping products
- Fire-resistant compartmentalization materials
