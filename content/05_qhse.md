## Section 2 · QHSE Introduction

**Section Title:** Quality, Health, Safety & Environment — Our Operational Backbone

At SS PLUS, QHSE is not a checkbox or a certification exercise. It is the management framework that governs how we run our business — how we manage our services, our people, our suppliers, and our environmental responsibilities.

Every SS PLUS process, from receiving a client request to delivering a field intervention, passes through defined QHSE checkpoints that ensure consistency, accountability, and continuous improvement. This is the same rigor we bring to every safety solution we design and deliver for our clients.

Our dedication to industry standards and compliance is a cornerstone of our mission. We believe that strict adherence to these standards not only ensures the safety of our clients but also contributes to the overall advancement of industrial safety practices across Tunisia.

## Section 3 · The Four QHSE Pillars

### Q — Quality Management

Standardized service delivery processes, documented procedures, supplier qualification protocols, client satisfaction monitoring, and structured internal audit cycles — all working together to ensure every deliverable meets our quality commitments. Quality management at SS PLUS is not audited once a year. It is built into every service interaction.

**Our quality approach includes:**
- Documented service delivery procedures for every service line
- Supplier qualification and product certification requirements
- Client satisfaction monitoring and feedback integration
- Internal audit cycles with corrective and preventive action tracking
- ISO 9001 certified management system (achieved 2010)

### H — Occupational Health

The physical and mental wellbeing of our team is a genuine priority, not a compliance formality. We apply workplace health policies, ergonomic practices, medical follow-up protocols, and clear exposure risk management — particularly for field technicians who work in challenging industrial environments.

**Our occupational health commitments:**
- Regular health monitoring for field-exposed team members
- Ergonomic guidelines for workshop and field operations
- Psychological wellbeing policies and open management culture
- Substance exposure protocols for technicians working with fire-fighting agents

### S — Safety Operations

All SS PLUS field operations comply with defined internal safety protocols. Before any field intervention, risks are assessed, PPE is confirmed, and safety briefings are conducted. We do not send our technicians into environments we haven't evaluated — and we apply to our own operations the same standards we recommend to our clients.

**Our safety operation protocols:**
- Site-specific risk assessments before every field intervention
- Mandatory PPE application for all field team members
- Toolbox talks before complex or high-risk operations
- Incident and near-miss reporting system with root cause analysis
- Emergency response procedures for all SS PLUS facilities and vehicles

### E — Environmental Responsibility

We manage our environmental footprint with intent and accountability — from the handling of fire-fighting agent refills and the disposal of safety materials, to the optimization of our logistics operations. We apply environmentally sound guidelines across all our activities.

**Our environmental practices:**
- Responsible handling and disposal of expired fire-fighting agents
- Managed waste disposal for condemned safety equipment
- Logistics optimization to minimize unnecessary vehicle movements
- Environmentally compliant packaging and material use

## Section 4 · Our QHSE Commitments

**Section Title:** What Our QHSE System Commits To — In Writing

1. Delivering services that fully meet client specifications, applicable regulatory standards, and contractual obligations — without exception.
2. Continuously improving our internal processes through audit findings, client feedback loops, and active benchmarking of industry best practices.
3. Ensuring zero-compromise on field technician safety during all service interventions — every team member returns home safely.
4. Maintaining full traceability on every product, inspection certificate, and maintenance record across every client account.
5. Operating with minimal and actively managed environmental impact in all logistical, workshop, and field activities.
6. Training and qualifying our team on an ongoing basis — technically, procedurally, and in terms of HSE behavior on-site.

## Section 5 · Standards & Certifications

**Section Title:** Standards We Reference & Certifications We Hold

At SS PLUS, our commitment to safety extends beyond our products and services to strict adherence to national and international industry standards. We take pride in upholding the highest level of compliance to ensure the effectiveness and reliability of our safety solutions.

### Certifications & Accreditations

**ISO 9001 — Quality Management Systems**  
SS PLUS has held ISO 9001 certification since 2010 — a hallmark of our commitment to quality management. This certification confirms that our processes for service delivery, client management, supplier qualification, and continuous improvement meet the rigorous requirements of the international standard.

**APSAD — Technical Reference**  
SS PLUS utilizes APSAD (Association pour la Protection des Sites et Ateliers Industriels) as a core technical reference for fire safety. APSAD certification and standards ensure that our products and services align with the best practices in fire safety and prevention — a benchmark recognized across France and francophone markets.

### Standards We Apply

| Standard | Domain |
|----------|--------|
| ISO 9001 | Quality Management Systems |
| APSAD Rules & Certifications | Fire Safety & Prevention |
| EN ISO 7010 | Safety Signs & Signage |
| NT Standards (Tunisia) | National Technical Requirements |
| EN 3 | Portable Fire Extinguishers |
| EN 671 | Fire-Fighting Hose Reels |
| EN 1869 | Fire Blankets |

### Stringent Quality Control

Our products undergo rigorous testing and quality control measures, meeting and often exceeding industry standards. This dedication to quality is reflected in every fire extinguisher, hose, fire-fighting system, and emergency response solution we provide. When SS PLUS certifies a product is compliant — it is compliant.
