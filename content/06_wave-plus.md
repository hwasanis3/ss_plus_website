## Section 2 · What Is WAVE PLUS

**Section Title:** The Safety Management Platform Built for SS PLUS Clients

Introduced in 2024, WAVE PLUS represents SS PLUS's most significant technological leap since its founding — and it positions us as the only industrial safety company in Tunisia offering this level of digital transparency and control to our clients.

WAVE PLUS is a web-based management application developed by the **WAVE IO** team. It gives SS PLUS and its clients a shared digital environment where every service interaction — every intervention, inspection, maintenance visit, contract, and piece of equipment — is logged, tracked, and accessible in real time.

For SS PLUS clients, WAVE PLUS means no more paper trails, no more lost certificates, no more phone calls to chase a maintenance status. For SS PLUS operations, it means higher service quality, faster response times, and complete traceability across every account we manage.

**This is not a client portal for its own sake. It is a tool that makes your business genuinely safer.**

## Section 3 · Platform Features — Client Portal

**Section Title:** Everything Your HSE Team Needs — In One Place.

<!-- [IMAGE] Annotated dashboard screenshot or icon-based feature grid -->

### 📋 Intervention Tracker
View all scheduled and completed service interventions across your sites — maintenance visits, periodic inspection rounds, emergency calls — with full details: status, assigned technician, date, duration, and outcome. No more calling to find out if your extinguisher has been serviced.

### 🧯 Equipment Registry
A complete, live digital inventory of all safety equipment managed by SS PLUS for your account — fire extinguishers, fire-fighting systems, hose reels, emergency equipment — with serial numbers, installation location, service history, and next scheduled maintenance date.

### 📅 Maintenance & Inspection Calendar
Your upcoming inspection and maintenance schedules, displayed clearly and filterable by site, equipment type, or date. Automatic alerts ensure you are notified before a regulatory deadline or certification renewal is missed — not after.

### 📄 Documents & Certificates
Download all your SS PLUS documentation directly from your dashboard — inspection reports, maintenance conformity certificates, intervention summaries, and contract documents. No more waiting for email attachments. Your full document library, accessible in seconds, ready for audits.

### 📦 Order & Supply Tracking
Track all PPE and equipment orders placed with SS PLUS — from confirmation through delivery. Full order history, delivery records, and product documentation, all on demand and clearly organized by date and site.

### 📊 Safety Compliance Dashboard
A visual summary of your account's current safety status — open interventions, upcoming service deadlines, equipment approaching maintenance, and overall compliance indicators. Designed for HSE managers who need a clear picture without navigating through files.

### 🔔 Alerts & Notifications
Automatic reminders for upcoming maintenance deadlines, expiring inspection certificates, pending interventions, and new documents added to your account. WAVE PLUS alerts you proactively — before a compliance issue becomes a problem.

### 💬 Direct Communication
Send requests, submit questions, and communicate directly with your SS PLUS account manager — all tracked, timestamped, and logged within the platform. A full, searchable communication history with your safety partner.
