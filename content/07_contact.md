## Section 7 · FAQ — Quick Answers

**Section Title:** Common Questions

**Do you serve clients outside of Sfax?**  
Yes. SS PLUS serves clients across all of Tunisia — from Tunis to Gabès, Monastir to Gafsa. Our intervention teams operate nationwide.

**How quickly can you respond to an inspection or maintenance request?**  
For scheduled services, we confirm appointments within 24 business hours. For emergency interventions, our 24/7 hotline connects you directly to our response team.

**Do you work with small and medium-sized businesses?**  
Absolutely. Our client portfolio ranges from large multinational operators to Tunisian SMEs. We tailor our proposals to your size, budget, and specific safety requirements.

**Can I request a site safety audit before committing to a service contract?**  
Yes. We offer initial site assessments as a starting point for all new client relationships. Contact us to schedule your visit.

**How do I access WAVE PLUS as an existing client?**  
Contact us at **waveio.tn@gmail.com** or call **+216 22 036 238** to activate your portal access. There is no additional cost for existing SS PLUS clients.
